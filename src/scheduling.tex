\section{Scheduling}
\subsection{Guaranteed schedule}
Utilisation factor: 
\[ U = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\frac{C_i}{T_i} \]
\begin{itemize}
    \item[] $C_i$: Cycle frequency of task i
    \item[] $T_i$: Worst case execution time (WCET) of task i
\end{itemize}
Utilisation bound: 
\[ U_b = n \cdot \left(2^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1\right) \]
Guaranteed schedule:
\[ U \leq U_b \]
Schedule possible:
\[ U \leq 1 \]
